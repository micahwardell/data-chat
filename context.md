# Additional Context

## Survey Scoring & Favorability

Survey items use a 1-5 scale. Results should be reported by **favorability**:

- **Favorable**: scores of 5 or 4
- **Neutral**: score of 3
- **Unfavorable**: scores of 2 or 1

When showing item results, always calculate and display the percentage of respondents in each favorability category (favorable, neutral, unfavorable) rather than raw averages, unless the user specifically asks for averages.
